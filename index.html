About me

I'm a programmer from Morocco who loves building things that solves life's unique problems.

Since a kid, I was interested at computers and everything related to tech. At age 8, I discovered videogames and I really wanted to make my own game, I first started with visual game creators (no coding), the quality of these game builders back then was so horrible that my wish of making a game disappeared. But then, it was revived at age 14, this time I started more technical, at that time, programming became more famous on the net, and that made me search about everything related to it. I started out with Python because back then, it was for me the easiest programming language. Unfortunetly, Python wasn't very suitable for game developping. And that's how my dream of making a good game was completely destroyed.

Now that I lost my time learning Python, I thought about searching what else can Python do, and that is when I found out about AI. It was until age 15 that I started an AI project. During that time, I was spending my time on making automation projects using the Raspberry Pi and Python, and making cool circuits with Arduino.

During the confinement, I learnt C++ and competitive programming primarly, then I started SQL and Git.

On the year 2021, I participated on the Moroccan Olympiads in Informatocs Juniors (MoiJ) made by an association, I ended up second. Later, that year, I participated on the international informatics olympiad, Lotfi Zadeh Olympiad, in which I represented Morocco. I'm currently looking forward to achieve more things in competitive programming.

I'm now seeking for every possible way to gain work experience and money, to invest more on learning.
